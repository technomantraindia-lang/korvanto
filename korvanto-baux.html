Overview

Product Overview

Korvanto LLP BAUX is a premium-grade calcined bauxite produced by carefully calcining high-quality bauxite ore at controlled temperatures to remove moisture and volatile compounds resulting in a dense hard and highly refractory material with elevated alumina content.

Renowned for its exceptional hardness thermal stability chemical resistance and low thermal expansion characteristics Korvanto LLP BAUX is widely used in refractory manufacturing abrasive applications welding consumables and other demanding industrial processes operating under extreme temperatures and abrasive conditions.

Our calcined bauxite grades are manufactured to provide consistent alumina content controlled impurities and reliable performance for steel plants cement kilns glass furnaces foundries refractory producers and abrasive manufacturers worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO BAUX
Korvanto LLP BAUX is a premium calcined bauxite produced by calcining high-grade bauxite ore at controlled temperatures. It is widely used as a refractory raw material due to its high alumina content excellent thermal stability mechanical strength and resistance to high-temperature environments.
What grades of KORVANTO BAUX are available
Korvanto LLP supplies three standard calcined bauxite grades: Korvanto LLP BAUX CB80 (Approx. 80% Al₂O₃) Korvanto LLP BAUX CB82 (Approx. 82% Al₂O₃) Korvanto LLP BAUX CB86 (Approx. 86% Al₂O₃) Each grade is designed to meet different refractory and high-temperature performance requirements.
How do I choose the right calcined bauxite grade
The selection depends on the required alumina content operating temperature refractory design and service conditions. CB80 is suitable for general refractory applications. CB82 is recommended for premium refractory products and high-strength castables. CB86 is intended for demanding high-temperature industrial applications requiring maximum refractoriness. Our technical team can recommend the most suitable grade based on your application.
What particle sizes are available
Korvanto LLP BAUX can be supplied in various sizes including: Powder (200 Mesh) Fine Aggregates Medium Aggregates Coarse Aggregates Custom particle size distributions can also be supplied according to customer requirements.
Which industries use KORVANTO BAUX
Korvanto LLP BAUX is supplied to refractory manufacturers steel plants cement plants foundries glass manufacturers and other high-temperature industries. Detailed applications are listed in the Applications section of this page.
Can Korvanto supply customized calcined bauxite specifications
Yes. Korvanto LLP can supply customized calcined bauxite grades with specific alumina content particle size distribution bulk density and other technical parameters based on customer and application requirements.
What packaging options are available

What quality documents are available
