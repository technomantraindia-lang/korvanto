Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BAUX is a premium-grade calcined bauxite produced by carefully calcining high-quality bauxite ore at controlled temperatures to remove moisture and volatile compounds, resulting in a dense, hard, and highly refractory material with elevated alumina content.

Renowned for its exceptional hardness, thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low thermal expansion characteristics, KORVANTO BAUX is widely used in refractory manufacturing, abrasive applications, welding consumables, and other demanding industrial processes operating under extreme temperatures and abrasive conditions.

Our calcined bauxite grades are manufactured to provide consistent alumina content, controlled impurities, and reliable performance for steel plants, cement kilns, glass furnaces, foundries, refractory producers, and abrasive manufacturers worldwide.
