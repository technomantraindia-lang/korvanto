Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026. A clear overview of how Korvanto LLP handles information shared through our website and business conversations.

Data Protection Your Privacy Matters

Who We Are

Korvanto LLP is a B2B industrial mineral exporter based in Rajkot, Gujarat, India. We supply bentonite, kaolin, ball clay, laterite, chamotte, calcined bauxite, and lustrous carbon additives to industrial buyers worldwide.

Our office is at 304, The City Centre, Near Amrapali Underbridge, Raiya Road, Rajkot, Gujarat, India.

What This Policy Covers

This policy covers information shared through our website forms, quote requests, documentation requests, sample requests, live chat, email, phone, and WhatsApp.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you contact us, we may collect:

  • Name, company name, job title, email address, and phone number
  • Country, destination port, and shipping preferences
  • Product interest, grade needs, estimated volume, packaging preference, and purchase timeline
  • Messages, notes, and files shared for quotation or technical discussion
  • Basic website data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Reply to enquiries and prepare quotations
  • Suggest suitable mineral grades and packaging options
  • Arrange quality documents and sample support
  • Manage export orders, dispatch planning, and customer communication
  • Keep business records needed for contracts, tax, and compliance
  • Improve website experience and support responses

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

When We Share Information

We share information only when needed with:

  • Our sales, quality, and logistics team
  • Trusted logistics, inspection, banking, or service partners helping complete your order
  • Authorities when the law requires disclosure

How Long We Keep Information

We keep enquiry and customer records for as long as needed to support the business relationship and meet legal and tax requirements. When records are no longer needed, we delete or securely archive them.

How We Protect Information

We take practical steps to protect information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Please avoid sending highly sensitive details through public forms unless necessary.

Your Choices

You can ask us to review, update, or remove personal information we hold about you. Write to us using the contact details below and we will help as quickly as we can.

Cookies and Website Tools

Our website may use basic cookies or similar tools for navigation, forms, and performance. If analytics tools are used, they help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

Links to Other Sites

Our website may link to platforms such as Google Maps, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, or WhatsApp. Those platforms follow their own privacy rules.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows the latest update.
