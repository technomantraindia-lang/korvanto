Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO DRILL is a range of high-quality drilling-grade bentonite products developed for oil & gas, water well, HDD, geothermal, piling, and geotechnical drilling applications. Available in API and OCMA grades, these products provide excellent viscosity, gel strength, borehole stability, fluid loss control, and cuttings suspension for efficient drilling operations worldwide.
