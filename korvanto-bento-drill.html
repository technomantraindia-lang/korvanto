Frequently Asked Questions

What is drilling grade bentonite?
Drilling grade bentonite is a specially processed bentonite designed to prepare drilling fluids used in oil & gas, water well, horizontal directional drilling (HDD), geothermal, and geotechnical drilling. It helps stabilize boreholes, suspend drill cuttings, control fluid loss, and improve drilling efficiency.
What drilling applications is KORVANTO BENTO DRILL™ suitable for?
Korvanto BENTO DRILL™ is suitable for a wide range of drilling applications, including: Oil & Gas Drilling Water Well Drilling Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) Geothermal Drilling Geotechnical Investigation Mineral & Core Exploration Borehole Stabilization The appropriate grade depends on drilling conditions and project requirements.
What is the difference between API and OCMA drilling bentonite?
API bentonite is manufactured to meet the requirements of API Specification 13A, commonly used in oil and gas drilling operations. OCMA bentonite is produced in accordance with OCMA specifications and is widely used for water well drilling, piling, HDD, and general drilling applications. The choice depends on the drilling method, fluid performance requirements, and customer specifications.
Does Korvanto offer API and OCMA grades?
Yes. Korvanto LLP offers a range of drilling bentonite products, including API Grade, OCMA Grade, and enhanced drilling grades. Customized drilling formulations can also be supplied based on customer requirements and application conditions.
What are the main functions of drilling bentonite?
Drilling bentonite performs several important functions, including: Stabilizing borehole walls Suspending and transporting drill cuttings Controlling fluid loss Cooling and lubricating drill bits Forming a low-permeability filter cake Supporting efficient drilling operations
Can drilling bentonite be customized?
Yes. Korvanto LLP can supply customized drilling bentonite grades based on customer specifications, drilling conditions, viscosity requirements, and application-specific performance requirements.

Yes. Selected drilling grades are suitable for HDD applications where drilling fluid performance, borehole stability, and efficient cuttings transport are essential. The recommended grade depends on soil conditions and project requirements.
Is KORVANTO BENTO DRILL™ suitable for water well drilling?
Yes. Korvanto BENTO DRILL™ is widely used in water well drilling applications to improve drilling fluid performance, stabilize boreholes, and facilitate efficient removal of drill cuttings.
What factors should be considered when selecting a drilling bentonite?
The appropriate drilling bentonite depends on several factors, including drilling method, formation type, borehole conditions, required viscosity, fluid loss characteristics, and applicable industry specifications. Korvanto LLP can assist in selecting the most suitable grade based on project requirements.
What is the shelf life of drilling bentonite?
When stored in a clean, dry environment and protected from moisture, drilling bentonite maintains its performance characteristics for an extended period. Proper storage in sealed packaging helps preserve product quality until use.
