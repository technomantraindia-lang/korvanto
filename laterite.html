Laterite

Iron-rich lateritic mineral used in cement production, road base, construction fill and selected industrial material formulations.

Overview

Laterite is a residual soil rich in iron and aluminium oxides, formed in tropical weathering environments. Korvanto supplies sized laterite for buyers requiring consistent chemistry for cement clinker supplementation and construction applications.

Key Properties

  • Elevated iron oxide content
  • Good binding characteristics when processed
  • Natural reddish-brown colour
  • Available in crushed and screened fractions

Cement raw mix additiveRoad base & sub-baseConstruction fillBrick & block manufacturing
