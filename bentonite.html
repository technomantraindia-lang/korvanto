Bentonite

High-plasticity swelling clay for drilling fluids, foundry bonding, civil engineering and sealant systems — supplied in export-ready bulk packaging.

Overview

Bentonite is a montmorillonite-rich clay exhibiting strong swelling and thixotropic behaviour in water. Korvanto supplies sodium and calcium bentonite grades for industrial buyers requiring consistent viscosity, gel strength and moisture content across export lots.

Key Properties

  • High swelling capacity in aqueous systems
  • Colloidal stability and viscosity control
  • Low permeability when hydrated for barrier applications
  • Configurable mesh size and moisture for handling
