Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO CARBO is a premium-quality lustrous carbon additive manufactured from carefully selected carbonaceous materials and processed to deliver superior performance in green sand molding systems. Designed specifically for ferrous foundries, it improves casting surface finish, reduces sand-related defects, and enhances molding sand performance.

When exposed to molten metal temperatures, KORVANTO CARBO generates a reducing gaseous atmosphere and forms a lustrous carbon layer at the mold-metal interface. This protective carbon film acts as a non-wetting barrier between molten metal and molding sand, minimizing metal penetration, sand adhesion, burn-on defects, and surface irregularities.

Engineered for modern foundry operations, KORVANTO CARBO helps improve casting quality, reduce rejection rates, enhance sand recyclability, and optimize overall production efficiency.
