Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO KAO CALCINED is a premium-grade anhydrous aluminum silicate produced by carefully calcining high-purity kaolin under controlled temperatures. The calcination process removes chemically bound water and modifies the kaolin crystal structure, resulting in enhanced whiteness, opacity, brightness, electrical insulation properties, thermal stability, and improved functional performance.

Engineered for demanding industrial applications, KORVANTO KAO CALCINED serves as a high-performance extender, filler, reinforcing agent, and pigment substitute in paints, coatings, plastics, rubber, paper, wire & cable compounds, printing inks, technical ceramics, and specialty formulations.

Its excellent dispersibility, low moisture content, controlled particle size distribution, and superior optical properties make it a preferred choice for manufacturers seeking improved product performance and cost optimization.
