Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO FOOD™ is a high-purity food-grade bentonite specially processed for beverage clarification purification and filtration applications. Predominantly composed of montmorillonite clay it possesses a naturally high adsorption capacity and negative surface charge that enables the effective removal of suspended particles proteins impurities and haze-forming compounds from liquid systems.

Widely used in wine fruit juice beverage edible oil and food processing industries KORVANTO BENTO FOOD™ improves product clarity stability appearance and shelf life while preserving the desired sensory characteristics of the final product. Its excellent swelling and adsorption properties make it an efficient and reliable clarifying agent for modern food and beverage processing operations.

Carefully processed under strict quality controls the product is designed to deliver consistent performance and reliable clarification efficiency across a variety of food-grade applications.

What is Food Grade Bentonite
Food Grade Bentonite is a highly purified bentonite intended for use in approved food processing and food-related industrial applications where controlled quality and product consistency are required. The suitability of the material depends on the intended application and applicable regulatory requirements.
What are the common applications of Food Grade Bentonite
Depending on local regulations and customer requirements Food Grade Bentonite may be used in selected food processing applications such as: Beverage Processing Edible Oil Processing Sugar Processing Food Ingredient Manufacturing Food Processing Industries The suitability for a specific application should always be verified against applicable food regulations.
