Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO KAO HYDROUS is a premium-grade water-washed kaolin produced from carefully selected deposits and processed to achieve high purity, excellent whiteness, controlled particle size, and superior dispersion characteristics. Composed primarily of naturally hydrated kaolinite, this product retains its original crystalline structure, making it an ideal functional filler, extender, and performance additive for a wide range of industrial applications.

Its fine particle size, chemical inertness, low abrasiveness, and high brightness make it suitable for paints, coatings, paper, plastics, rubber, printing inks, agrochemicals, ceramics, adhesives, and specialty formulations. KORVANTO KAO HYDROUS contributes to improved surface finish, opacity, gloss, printability, and mechanical performance while maintaining formulation stability.

Manufactured under strict quality controls, it delivers consistent performance and reliable results across diverse industries.
