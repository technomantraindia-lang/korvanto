Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO KAO SPRAY is a premium spray dried kaolin produced by atomizing refined kaolin slurry in a controlled drying process. The resulting granules offer uniform moisture content, excellent flow properties, and a consistent particle structure tailored for modern ceramic pressing and body formulation.

Designed for ceramic bodies, pressed tiles, and sanitaryware applications, this grade delivers reliable pressing performance, improved green strength, easy blending in ceramic formulations, and uniform composition for high-quality finished products.

Manufactured from a high-purity refined kaolin base with low impurity levels and strict quality controls, KORVANTO KAO SPRAY supports efficient ceramic production, consistent batch-to-batch performance, and export-ready supply for tile and sanitaryware manufacturers worldwide.
