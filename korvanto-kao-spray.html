Overview

Product Overview

Korvanto KAO SPRAY is a premium spray dried kaolin produced by atomizing refined kaolin slurry in a controlled drying process. The resulting granules offer uniform moisture content excellent flow properties and a consistent particle structure tailored for modern ceramic pressing and body formulation.

Designed for ceramic bodies pressed tiles and sanitaryware applications this grade delivers reliable pressing performance improved green strength easy blending in ceramic formulations and uniform composition for high-quality finished products.

Manufactured from a high-purity refined kaolin base with low impurity levels and strict quality controls Korvanto KAO SPRAY supports efficient ceramic production consistent batch-to-batch performance and export-ready supply for tile and sanitaryware manufacturers worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO KAO SPRAY used for
Korvanto KAO SPRAY is a spray-dried kaolin specially processed for ceramic body preparation. It provides consistent moisture excellent flowability and uniform particle distribution making it ideal for ceramic tiles sanitaryware porcelain products ceramic glazes and other whiteware applications.
What are the advantages of spray-dried kaolin
Spray-dried kaolin offers excellent flow characteristics uniform granule size consistent moisture content improved handling and better press feeding helping manufacturers achieve uniform ceramic body preparation and consistent production quality.
Which industries and applications use KORVANTO KAO SPRAY
Korvanto KAO SPRAY is suitable for: Ceramic Bodies Pressed Tiles Sanitaryware Wall Tiles Floor Tiles Vitrified Tiles Porcelain Ware Tableware Wash Basins Water Closets (WC) Sanitary Ceramic Bodies Ceramic Glazes Engobes
Why is spray-dried kaolin preferred in ceramic manufacturing
Spray-dried kaolin provides uniform granule size and controlled moisture allowing better die filling improved pressing consistency reduced dust generation and more uniform green body density during ceramic manufacturing.
Can Korvanto customize spray-dried kaolin specifications
Yes. Korvanto can supply spray-dried kaolin with application-specific specifications including moisture content particle size distribution bulk density and other quality parameters according to customer manufacturing requirements.
