About Korvanto Kaolin (China Clay)

Korvanto Kaolin (China Clay) includes a complete range of natural, processed, and calcined kaolin products manufactured for ceramic, paint, paper, rubber, plastic, construction, refractory, and specialty industrial applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is kaolin?
Kaolin, also known as china clay, is a naturally occurring hydrated aluminum silicate mineral valued for its high whiteness, fine particle size, chemical stability, and excellent processing characteristics. It is widely used across ceramics, paper, paints, plastics, rubber, construction, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and many other industrial applications.
