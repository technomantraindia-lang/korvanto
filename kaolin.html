Kaolin / China Clay

Fine white kaolin for ceramic bodies, paper coating, rubber compounding and industrial filler applications — with brightness and particle size control.

Overview

Kaolin (china clay) is a hydrated aluminium silicate valued for whiteness, low iron content and controlled particle distribution. Korvanto supplies washed and calcined kaolin grades for B2B programmes requiring stable rheology and fired colour.

Key Properties

  • High brightness and low grit
  • Controlled particle size distribution
  • Low resin demand in coating applications
  • Good suspension and dispersion characteristics
