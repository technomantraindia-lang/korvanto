Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO FERT™ is a premium-grade sodium bentonite specially developed for fertilizer and agricultural applications. Its unique combination of high swelling capacity moisture retention cation exchange capability and binding properties makes it an effective additive for fertilizer granulation nutrient retention and soil improvement.

Used extensively in NPK DAP urea sulfur bentonite micronutrient organic and bio-fertilizer formulations KORVANTO BENTO FERT™ improves granule strength reduces dust generation enhances fertilizer efficiency and promotes controlled nutrient release. It also acts as a natural soil conditioner by improving water-holding capacity soil structure and nutrient availability.

As an eco-friendly mineral additive it supports sustainable agricultural practices while helping maximize fertilizer performance and crop productivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO BENTO FERTILIZER used for
Korvanto BENTO FERTILIZER is a premium sodium bentonite used as a natural binder carrier and moisture-retention agent in fertilizer manufacturing. It improves granule strength reduces dust formation enhances nutrient distribution and supports the production of durable fertilizer granules for both organic and inorganic fertilizer applications.
What is the difference between AG-100 AG-200 and AG-300
Korvanto BENTO FERTILIZER is available in three grades to suit different fertilizer manufacturing processes. AG-100 – Standard Fertilizer Grade is suitable for general fertilizer blending and standard granulation applications. AG-200 – Granulation Grade provides improved binding efficiency better granule formation and enhanced production performance for granulated fertilizers. AG-300 – Premium Granule Binder Grade delivers superior granule strength reduced dust generation and excellent durability for high-performance fertilizer manufacturing. The appropriate grade depends on the fertilizer formulation production process and required granule quality.
Which fertilizer applications are suitable for KORVANTO BENTO FERTILIZER
Korvanto BENTO FERTILIZER is suitable for: NPK Fertilizers Organic Fertilizers Bio Fertilizers Granulated Fertilizers Micronutrient Fertilizers Bentonite Sulphur Fertilizers Soil Conditioner Granules Controlled Release Fertilizers Mineral-Based Fertilizers Specialty Fertilizer Products
Can fertilizer-grade bentonite be customized for different production processes
Yes. Korvanto can supply customized fertilizer-grade bentonite according to fertilizer formulation granulation equipment binder requirements particle size moisture content and manufacturing process to achieve optimum granule quality and production efficiency.
