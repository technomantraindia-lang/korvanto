Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO FERT is a premium-grade sodium bentonite specially developed for fertilizer and agricultural applications. Its unique combination of high swelling capacity, moisture retention, cation exchange capability, and binding properties makes it an effective additive for fertilizer granulation, nutrient retention, and soil improvement.

Used extensively in NPK, DAP, urea, sulfur bentonite, micronutrient, organic, and bio-fertilizer formulations, KORVANTO BENTO FERT improves granule strength, reduces dust generation, enhances fertilizer efficiency, and promotes controlled nutrient release. It also acts as a natural soil conditioner by improving water-holding capacity, soil structure, and nutrient availability.

As an eco-friendly mineral additive, it supports sustainable agricultural practices while helping maximize fertilizer performance and crop productivity.
