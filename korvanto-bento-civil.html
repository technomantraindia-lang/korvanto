Overview

Product Overview

Korvanto BENTO CIVIL is a premium-grade sodium bentonite specially engineered for civil engineering foundation and construction projects. Processed from high-quality bentonite and optimized for superior rheological performance it develops a stable thixotropic slurry that provides excellent excavation support borehole stabilization and seepage control.

When mixed with water it forms a high-yield suspension capable of penetrating soil voids sealing permeable formations and creating a protective filter cake that stabilizes excavation walls. Its exceptional water absorption swelling capacity and gel strength make it ideal for demanding piling trenching tunneling and foundation applications.

Designed for modern construction projects Korvanto BENTO CIVIL delivers reliable performance improved operational efficiency and reduced excavation risks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO BENTO CIVIL used for
Korvanto BENTO CIVIL is a high-performance sodium bentonite specially processed for civil engineering and foundation construction. It is used to prepare bentonite slurry that stabilizes excavations minimizes filtration loss supports borehole walls and improves drilling efficiency in piling diaphragm wall tunneling and trenching projects.
What is the difference between C-33 and C-45
The primary difference is the Marsh Cone slurry viscosity which determines slurry performance for different construction conditions. Korvanto BENTO CIVIL C-33 (Minimum 33-second Marsh Cone viscosity) is designed for general civil engineering applications such as bored piling slurry trenching and standard foundation works. Korvanto BENTO CIVIL C-45 (Minimum 45-second Marsh Cone viscosity) provides higher viscosity lower filtration loss improved suspension and greater borehole stability for demanding applications such as diaphragm walls tunnel boring (TBM) horizontal directional drilling (HDD) microtunneling and difficult ground conditions.
Which civil engineering applications are suitable for KORVANTO BENTO CIVIL
Korvanto BENTO CIVIL is suitable for a wide range of civil engineering applications including: Bored Pile Foundations Diaphragm Wall Construction Slurry Trenching Tunnel Boring Machines (TBM) Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) Microtunneling Shaft Excavation Cut-off Walls Foundation Engineering Ground Stabilization Projects
Can the slurry properties be customized for specific projects
Yes. Different soil conditions groundwater levels drilling equipment and project specifications require different slurry characteristics. Korvanto LLP can supply customized civil-grade bentonite with tailored viscosity filtration loss barrel yield particle size and other performance parameters to meet project-specific requirements.
