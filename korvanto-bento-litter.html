Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO LITTER is a high-quality natural bentonite specially processed for cat litter applications. With exceptional water absorption, rapid clumping ability, and natural odor control properties, it provides a clean, hygienic, and convenient litter solution for pet care manufacturers and private label brands.

When exposed to moisture, the bentonite granules quickly absorb liquid and form strong, stable clumps that can be easily removed while leaving the remaining litter fresh and reusable. Its superior absorption capacity helps control odors, reduce waste, and maintain a cleaner litter box environment.

Available in various granule sizes and customized formulations, KORVANTO BENTO LITTER can be supplied with or without fragrances to meet diverse market requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cat Litter Grade Bentonite?
Cat Litter Grade Bentonite is a specially processed bentonite designed for pet hygiene applications. It is valued for its high absorbency, excellent clumping performance, odor control properties, and ease of cleaning, making it one of the most widely used materials in premium cat litter products.
Why is bentonite used in cat litter?
Bentonite naturally absorbs moisture and forms firm clumps when wet, allowing waste to be removed easily while helping keep the remaining litter clean. Its absorbent properties also contribute to improved odor control and longer litter life.
