Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO CHAM™ is a premium-grade calcined refractory clay (Chamotte) produced from carefully selected kaolinitic clays and fired at high temperatures to develop exceptional thermal stability strength and dimensional consistency. The calcination process transforms the clay into a highly stable refractory aggregate capable of withstanding extreme temperatures and thermal cycling.

Processed through advanced crushing screening and grading systems KORVANTO CHAM™ is available in various particle sizes and alumina grades to meet the requirements of refractory manufacturers ceramic producers foundries steel plants cement kilns and other high-temperature industries.

Its superior resistance to thermal shock abrasion shrinkage and chemical degradation makes it an ideal raw material for fire bricks castables refractory mortars kiln furniture insulation materials and technical ceramics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO CHAM
Korvanto CHAM is a high-quality calcined refractory clay (chamotte) produced by firing selected fireclays at controlled temperatures. It is widely used in refractory ceramic kiln furniture and high-temperature industrial applications where thermal stability and consistent performance are required.
What grades of KORVANTO CHAM are available
Korvanto supplies five standard chamotte grades: Korvanto CHAM LF42 Korvanto CHAM LF46 Korvanto CHAM LF54 Korvanto CHAM LF60 Korvanto CHAM LF70 Each grade is designed to meet different alumina content and refractory performance requirements.
How do I select the right chamotte grade
The appropriate grade depends on the required alumina content service temperature refractory design and end-use application. Our technical team can recommend the most suitable grade based on your manufacturing process and performance requirements.
What particle sizes are available
Korvanto CHAM is available in various particle sizes including: Powder (200 Mesh) 0–1 mm 1–3 mm 3–5 mm 5–8 mm Custom sizing can also be supplied based on customer requirements.
Can Korvanto supply customized chamotte specifications
Yes. Korvanto can supply customized chamotte grades with specific alumina content particle size distribution bulk density and other technical parameters according to customer and application requirements.
Which industries use KORVANTO CHAM
Korvanto CHAM is supplied for refractory manufacturing ceramics kiln furniture foundries furnaces and other high-temperature industrial applications. Detailed end-use industries are listed in the Applications section of this page.
What packaging options are available
Korvanto CHAM is available in: 25 kg Bags 50 kg Bags Jumbo Bags (FIBC) Palletized Shipments Bulk Container Loads Customized export packaging is also available upon request.
What quality documents are available
Depending on customer requirements Korvanto can provide: Technical Data Sheet (TDS) Certificate of Analysis (COA) Safety Data Sheet (SDS) where applicable Commercial Invoice Packing List Certificate of Origin (where applicable) Other export documentation as required
Does Korvanto export chamotte internationally
Yes. Korvanto supplies export-ready chamotte with quality verification secure packaging and complete export documentation to customers worldwide.
