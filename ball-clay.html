Ball Clay

Fine-grained plastic clay enhancing workability, green strength and fired properties in ceramic body formulations.

Overview

Ball clay is a sedimentary kaolinitic clay with high plasticity and fine particle size. Korvanto supplies blends suitable for sanitaryware, tiles and technical ceramics where rheology and fired strength are critical.

Key Properties

  • High plasticity and binding power
  • Fine particle size for smooth surfaces
  • Controlled organic content
  • Compatible with kaolin and feldspar bodies

Applications

Ceramic body plasticity Sanitaryware Floor & wall tiles Tableware Refractory plastic mixes
