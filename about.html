About Us

About Korvanto LLP

Korvanto LLP is the export division of Shree Shakti Enterprise, a well-established mineral supply company with over a decade of experience in mineral procurement, quality management, transportation, and supply chain coordination across India.

Built on the foundation of Shree Shakti Enterprise's industry expertise, Korvanto LLP was established to serve international industrial buyers with reliable access to quality industrial minerals and raw materials. Through a structured sourcing and quality-focused approach, Korvanto supports customers from material selection and specification verification to export packaging, logistics, and global dispatch.

Our Heritage

Our Background

For more than 10 years, Shree Shakti Enterprise has supplied minerals to industries across India, developing strong relationships with producers, processors, transporters, and industrial consumers. This experience has provided deep insight into material sourcing, quality requirements, logistics management, and customer expectations across a wide range of industrial applications.

Korvanto LLP represents the natural evolution of this expertise into international markets, bringing the same commitment to quality, consistency, reliability, and customer service to industrial buyers worldwide.
