Packaging Formats

Korvanto supplies minerals in formats suited to industrial handling and maritime logistics. Standard options include:

  • Jumbo bags (FIBC) — typically 500 kg—1,000 kg, UV-stabilised where required
  • Multi-wall paper / PP sacks — 25 kg and 50 kg units for controlled dosing
  • Bulk container loads — loose fill in lined containers for high-volume programmes
