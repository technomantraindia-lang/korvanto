Bulk Packaging Options

Korvanto supplies minerals in formats suited to industrial handling and maritime logistics — configured to customer requirements.

  • 25 kg bags — multi-wall paper / PP sacks for controlled dosing
  • 50 kg bags — standard industrial sack units for bulk handling
  • Jumbo bags (FIBC) — typically 500 kg—1,000 kg, UV-stabilised where required
  • Containerized loads — bulk fill in lined containers for high-volume programmes
