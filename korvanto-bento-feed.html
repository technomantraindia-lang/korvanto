Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO FEED is a high-purity bentonite specially processed for use in livestock, poultry, aquaculture, and animal nutrition applications. With excellent adsorption capacity, high cation exchange properties, and natural binding characteristics, it functions as an effective mycotoxin binder, pellet binder, and anti-caking agent in animal feed formulations.

Designed to improve feed quality and safety, KORVANTO BENTO FEED helps reduce the harmful effects of mycotoxins such as aflatoxins, supports feed stability during storage and handling, and enhances pellet durability. Its natural detoxifying properties contribute to improved feed efficiency, better nutrient utilization, and overall animal performance.

Suitable for use in cattle, poultry, swine, sheep, goat, and aquaculture feed, KORVANTO BENTO FEED is manufactured under strict quality controls to ensure consistent performance and low impurity levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Animal Feed Grade Bentonite?
Animal Feed Grade Bentonite is a specially processed bentonite used as a functional feed additive and pellet binder in animal nutrition. It is valued for its adsorption, binding, anti-caking, and flow-enhancing properties, making it suitable for use in commercial feed manufacturing.
Why is bentonite used in animal feed?
Bentonite is commonly used in animal feed to improve pellet quality, reduce feed caking, enhance feed flowability, support uniform ingredient distribution, and provide adsorption properties that can help improve overall feed handling and manufacturing efficiency.
Which animals can benefit from Animal Feed Grade Bentonite?
KORVANTO BENTO FEED™ is suitable for a wide range of feed applications, including: Dairy Cattle Beef Cattle Poultry Swine Sheep & Goats Aquaculture Pet Food Feed Premixes The suitability and inclusion level should always be determined according to the specific formulation and applicable regulations.
Can bentonite be used as a pellet binder?
Yes. Bentonite is widely used as a natural pellet binder in compound feed manufacturing. It helps improve pellet durability, reduces fines generation during handling, and contributes to consistent pellet quality.
Does Animal Feed Grade Bentonite help with mycotoxin management?
Certain grades of bentonite are widely used for their adsorption properties and may help reduce the effects of specific mycotoxins in feed formulations. The suitability of a particular grade depends on the target application, feed composition, and applicable regulatory requirements.
