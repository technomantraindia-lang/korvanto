Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO FEED is a high-purity bentonite specially processed for use in livestock, poultry, aquaculture, and animal nutrition applications. With excellent adsorption capacity, high cation exchange properties, and natural binding characteristics, it functions as an effective mycotoxin binder, pellet binder, and anti-caking agent in animal feed formulations.

Designed to improve feed quality and safety, KORVANTO BENTO FEED helps reduce the harmful effects of mycotoxins such as aflatoxins, supports feed stability during storage and handling, and enhances pellet durability. Its natural detoxifying properties contribute to improved feed efficiency, better nutrient utilization, and overall animal performance.

Suitable for use in cattle, poultry, swine, sheep, goat, and aquaculture feed, KORVANTO BENTO FEED is manufactured under strict quality controls to ensure consistent performance and low impurity levels.
