Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO KAO LEVIGATED NOODLES™ are premium-grade water-washed and refined kaolin products manufactured through a controlled levigation process that removes grit iron-bearing impurities and other unwanted minerals. Supplied in noodle form with controlled moisture content these grades offer excellent plasticity workability whiteness and consistency required by modern ceramic manufacturers.

Designed for ceramic bodies sanitaryware vitrified tiles porcelain tableware and refractory applications the product provides superior molding characteristics improved green strength and enhanced fired properties. Its uniform composition and low impurity levels contribute to better production efficiency and high-quality finished products.

Manufactured under controlled processing conditions to ensure consistent whiteness plasticity and low impurity levels across every batch.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO KAO NOODLES used for
Korvanto KAO NOODLES are refined levigated kaolin noodles primarily used in ceramic manufacturing. They provide a clean consistent raw material for wall tiles floor tiles vitrified tiles sanitaryware porcelain products ceramic glazes and other white ceramic applications.
Why choose levigated kaolin noodles instead of crude kaolin
Levigated kaolin noodles undergo washing and beneficiation to remove impurities and improve consistency. Compared to crude kaolin they offer better plasticity cleaner mineral composition improved workability and more consistent performance in ceramic production.
Which industries and applications use KORVANTO KAO NOODLES
Korvanto KAO NOODLES are suitable for: Ceramic Industry Wall Tiles Floor Tiles Vitrified Tiles Porcelain Tiles Slab Tiles Sanitaryware Wash Basins Water Closets (WC) Sanitary Ceramic Bodies Whiteware & Tableware Bone China Porcelain Ware Crockery Tableware Stoneware Ceramic Glazes Engobes Refractory Products Ceramic Insulators
What are the advantages of supplying kaolin in noodle form
The noodle form offers easier handling uniform moisture distribution reduced dust generation and convenient storage before further processing in ceramic manufacturing.
Can Korvanto customize the moisture level or product characteristics
Yes. Korvanto can supply levigated kaolin noodles with application-specific characteristics such as moisture level plasticity particle size and other quality parameters based on customer requirements.
