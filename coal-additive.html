Coal Additive / Lustrous Coal

Specialised carbonaceous additive for foundry facing sand, mould coatings and metallurgical processes requiring controlled volatiles and lustre.

Overview

Coal additive (lustrous coal) is a finely processed carbon material used to improve surface finish and peel of castings in foundry operations. Korvanto supplies material with controlled particle size and volatile matter for consistent mould performance.

Key Properties

  • Defined volatile matter and fixed carbon
  • Fine particle size for uniform coating
  • Good lustre and release characteristics
  • Low ash where premium grades required

Applications

Foundry facing sand Mould & core coatings Green sand additives Refractory carbon additions Metallurgical blend components
