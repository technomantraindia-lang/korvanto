Frequently Asked Questions

What are Specialty Bentonite Solutions
Specialty Bentonite Solutions are application-specific bentonite products supplied for niche industrial processes where standard bentonite grades may not meet the required technical or performance specifications.
What specialty bentonite applications does Korvanto support
Korvanto supplies bentonite solutions for a variety of specialized applications including: Acid Activated Bleaching Earth Water Treatment Pesticide & Agrochemical Carrier Ceramic Industry Wine Clarification Other application-specific industrial requirements Additional grades may be supplied based on customer requirements.
Can Korvanto source grades that are not listed on the website
Yes. If your required application is not listed our team can evaluate your technical requirements and work with qualified manufacturing partners to identify or source a suitable bentonite grade.
Do you manufacture specialty bentonite
Korvanto is an international supplier and exporter of industrial minerals. We work with qualified manufacturing partners to supply application-specific bentonite solutions that meet customer specifications and export requirements.
Can bentonite specifications be customized
Yes. Depending on the application customized specifications such as particle size moisture content swelling characteristics adsorption properties and other technical parameters can be supplied subject to feasibility.
What information should I provide when requesting a custom grade
To help us recommend the most suitable solution please provide: Intended application Technical specifications (if available) Quantity required Destination country Packaging preference Any applicable industry or quality requirements
What industries can benefit from Specialty Bentonite Solutions
Our specialty bentonite products are supplied to industries such as: Edible Oil Processing Chemical Manufacturing Water Treatment Agriculture Ceramics Beverage Processing Environmental Applications Other industrial sectors requiring application-specific bentonite.
What technical documents are available
Korvanto can provide: Technical Data Sheet (TDS) Certificate of Analysis (COA) Safety Data Sheet (SDS/MSDS) where applicable Export documentation upon request
Can Korvanto supply export quantities
Yes. We supply specialty bentonite products worldwide with export-ready packaging documentation support and flexible shipment options.
Is third-party inspection available
Yes. Independent third-party inspection and testing can be arranged upon customer request prior to shipment.
What packaging options are available
Packaging options include: 25 kg Bags 50 kg Bags Jumbo Bags (FIBC) Palletized Loads Customized Export Packaging Packaging can be tailored to customer requirements.
How can I request a custom bentonite solution
Simply contact the Korvanto export team through our website email WhatsApp or the Discuss Your Application form. Share your application technical requirements quantity destination country and packaging preference. Our team will review your requirements and recommend the most suitable bentonite solution.
