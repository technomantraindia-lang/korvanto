Overview

Product Overview

Korvanto Ball Clay is a high-quality, fine-grained clay renowned for its exceptional plasticity, strong binding characteristics, and excellent workability. Composed primarily of kaolinite along with naturally occurring quartz and mica, it serves as a critical raw material in ceramic, sanitaryware, porcelain, tile, refractory, and technical ceramic applications.

Carefully selected and processed to ensure consistency, Korvanto Ball Clay enhances shaping, molding, casting, and extrusion performance while providing excellent green strength and reliable firing characteristics. Its superior plasticity allows manufacturers to produce complex shapes, thin-walled products, and high-quality ceramic bodies with improved durability and surface finish.

Designed for modern ceramic manufacturing, it offers consistent performance across a wide range of industrial applications.

What makes ball clay different from kaolin?
Although both are ceramic clays, they serve different purposes. Ball clay offers high plasticity, strong binding properties, and excellent green strength. Kaolin provides higher whiteness, lower plasticity, and improved fired properties. Many ceramic formulations use both materials together to achieve the desired balance of workability and finished product performance.
