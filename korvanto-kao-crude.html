Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO KAO CRUDE™ is a naturally occurring kaolin clay (China Clay) selectively mined and supplied in its raw unprocessed form. Composed primarily of hydrated aluminum silicate it is valued for its natural whiteness plasticity fine particle structure and low abrasiveness making it an important raw material across numerous industrial applications.

Carefully sourced to ensure consistent quality KORVANTO KAO CRUDE™ serves as a versatile mineral for ceramic bodies paints paper rubber plastics sanitaryware and construction materials. Its excellent workability firing characteristics and filler properties contribute to improved product performance and manufacturing efficiency.

Available in dried lump form the material is suitable for further processing grinding levigation calcination or direct industrial use depending on application requirements.

Can Crude Kaolin be processed into refined grades
Yes. Crude Kaolin serves as the base raw material for manufacturing refined kaolin products such as levigated kaolin hydrous kaolin spray-dried kaolin calcined kaolin and metakaolin. The choice depends on the required purity brightness particle size and end-use application.
