Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO KAO LEVIGATED LUMPS are high-quality water-refined kaolin lumps produced through an advanced levigation process that removes coarse particles, iron impurities, and unwanted minerals. The resulting product offers enhanced whiteness, improved purity, controlled moisture content, and superior processing characteristics.

Supplied in lump form for further grinding, milling, or industrial processing, these grades are widely used by ceramic manufacturers, paint producers, rubber compounders, white cement manufacturers, and specialty mineral processors seeking consistent quality and reliable performance.
