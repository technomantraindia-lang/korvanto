Product Overview

Korvanto LLP KAO LEVIGATED LUMPS are high-quality water-refined kaolin lumps produced through an advanced levigation process that removes coarse particles, iron impurities, and unwanted minerals. The resulting product offers enhanced whiteness, improved purity, controlled moisture content, and superior processing characteristics.

Supplied in lump form for further grinding, milling, or industrial processing, these grades are widely used by ceramic manufacturers, paint producers, rubber compounders, white cement manufacturers, and specialty mineral processors seeking consistent quality and reliable performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO KAO LUMPS used for?
Korvanto LLP KAO LUMPS are refined levigated kaolin supplied in lump form for ceramic manufacturing and industrial mineral applications. They are widely used in ceramic bodies, sanitaryware, glazes, paints, polymers, rubber, construction materials, mineral processing, and further beneficiation operations.
Why choose levigated kaolin lumps instead of crude kaolin?
Levigated kaolin lumps are produced through washing and beneficiation processes that remove impurities and improve consistency. Compared to crude kaolin, they offer better purity, improved plasticity, more uniform mineral composition, and enhanced performance in ceramic and industrial applications.
Which industries and applications use KORVANTO KAO LUMPS?
Korvanto LLP KAO LUMPS are suitable for: Ceramic Industry Ceramic Bodies Glaze Manufacturing Sanitaryware Wall & Floor Tiles Porcelain Products Paint Industry Paint Undercoats Industrial Coatings Decorative Paints Polymer Industry Rubber Compounds Plastic Compounds Construction Materials White Cement Specialty Building Materials Mineral Processing Kaolin Powder Manufacturing Further Beneficiation Operations
Why are levigated kaolin lumps preferred for further processing?
The lump form is ideal for customers who carry out their own crushing, grinding, or milling operations. It offers flexibility for downstream processing while maintaining the quality and consistency achieved through the levigation process.
Can Korvanto customize lump size and quality specifications?
Yes. Korvanto LLP can supply levigated kaolin lumps in customized lump sizes and application-specific specifications, including moisture level, plasticity, particle characteristics, and other quality requirements.
