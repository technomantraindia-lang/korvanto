Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO PENCIL is a premium-grade bentonite specially developed for the pencil manufacturing industry. Produced from carefully selected bentonite deposits with high montmorillonite content, it serves as an effective binder in the production of pencil leads, providing the strength, cohesion, and workability required during extrusion, drying, and firing processes.

Its superior binding characteristics, high water absorption capacity, and excellent green and dry strength properties help produce pencil leads with consistent shape, smooth writing performance, and reduced breakage. The fine particle size and high surface area enable efficient bonding of graphite and other lead-forming materials while maintaining dimensional stability throughout manufacturing.

KORVANTO BENTO PENCIL is designed to support efficient production, improved lead quality, and reduced rejection rates in modern pencil manufacturing operations.
