Overview

Product Overview

Korvanto BENTO PENCIL is a premium-grade bentonite specially developed for the pencil manufacturing industry. Produced from carefully selected bentonite deposits with high montmorillonite content, it serves as an effective binder in the production of pencil leads, providing the strength, cohesion, and workability required during extrusion, drying, and firing processes.

Its superior binding characteristics, high water absorption capacity, and excellent green and dry strength properties help produce pencil leads with consistent shape, smooth writing performance, and reduced breakage. The fine particle size and high surface area enable efficient bonding of graphite and other lead-forming materials while maintaining dimensional stability throughout manufacturing.

Korvanto BENTO PENCIL is designed to support efficient production, improved lead quality, and reduced rejection rates in modern pencil manufacturing operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO BENTO PENCIL used for?
Korvanto BENTO PENCIL is a high-purity sodium bentonite specially processed for the pencil manufacturing industry. It is used as a binder in pencil lead formulations, helping improve extrusion, green strength, dimensional stability, and the overall quality of graphite and colored pencil cores.
What types of pencils is KORVANTO BENTO PENCIL suitable for?
Korvanto BENTO PENCIL is suitable for: Graphite Pencils HB & Writing Pencils Drawing Pencils Colored Pencils Sketching Pencils Artist Pencils School Stationery Pencils Specialty Pencil Lead Formulations
What are the benefits of using Pencil Grade Bentonite?
Korvanto BENTO PENCIL offers: Excellent binding properties Smooth extrusion during pencil lead manufacturing Improved green strength before firing or drying Uniform lead density and consistency Reduced breakage during production Better dimensional stability Consistent manufacturing performance
Can Pencil Grade Bentonite be customized for different pencil formulations?
Yes. Korvanto LLP can supply customized pencil-grade bentonite based on particle size, plasticity, moisture content, swelling characteristics, and formulation requirements to suit different graphite, clay, and colored pencil manufacturing processes.
