Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO COSMETIC is a premium-quality bentonite specially processed for cosmetic, skincare, haircare, and personal care applications. Derived from carefully selected natural bentonite deposits, it offers excellent absorption, adsorption, thickening, suspension, and rheological properties that make it a versatile ingredient in a wide range of beauty and personal care formulations.

Rich in naturally occurring minerals, KORVANTO BENTO COSMETIC helps absorb excess oil, impurities, and surface contaminants while improving product texture, stability, and application performance. Its fine particle size and smooth dispersion characteristics make it suitable for facial masks, cleansers, creams, lotions, shampoos, hair masks, and color cosmetic formulations.

Widely used by cosmetic manufacturers, the product contributes to enhanced formulation stability, improved sensory feel, and consistent product quality.
