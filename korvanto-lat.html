Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO LAT is a naturally occurring iron and alumina-rich lateritic mineral selectively mined and processed for use in cement manufacturing, construction materials, road infrastructure, and industrial applications. Formed through prolonged weathering of rocks in tropical environments, laterite offers excellent durability, weather resistance, and consistent chemical composition.

With its high iron and alumina content, KORVANTO LAT serves as a valuable corrective material in cement raw mix formulations while also finding applications in road construction, building materials, landscaping, and environmental projects. Available in lump, crushed, or powder form, it provides a cost-effective and sustainable mineral solution for large-scale industrial and infrastructure developments.
