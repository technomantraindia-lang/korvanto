Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO LATERITE™ is a naturally occurring iron and alumina-rich lateritic mineral selectively mined and processed for use in cement manufacturing construction materials road infrastructure and industrial applications. Formed through prolonged weathering of rocks in tropical environments laterite offers excellent durability weather resistance and consistent chemical composition.

With its high iron and alumina content KORVANTO LATERITE™ serves as a valuable corrective material in cement raw mix formulations while also finding applications in road construction building materials landscaping and environmental projects. Available in lump crushed or powder form it provides a cost-effective and sustainable mineral solution for large-scale industrial and infrastructure developments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KORVANTO LATER
Korvanto LATER is a naturally occurring laterite material rich in iron and alumina. It is processed for use in cement manufacturing construction infrastructure development environmental applications and other industrial uses requiring consistent raw material quality.
Which industries use KORVANTO LATER
Korvanto LATER is supplied to cement manufacturers construction companies infrastructure projects environmental solution providers and other industrial sectors. Specific end-use applications are listed in the Applications section of this page.
Why is laterite used in the cement industry
Laterite is commonly used as a corrective raw material in cement manufacturing because it provides naturally occurring iron and alumina required for clinker production and raw mix balancing.
Can KORVANTO LATER be supplied for construction and infrastructure projects
Yes. Korvanto LATER can be supplied for construction materials road and infrastructure projects embankments ground stabilization and other engineering applications subject to project specifications.
Is KORVANTO LATER suitable for environmental applications
Yes. Selected laterite grades can be supplied for environmental and filtration applications including iron removal systems and water filtration media depending on the required specifications.
Can Korvanto supply customized laterite specifications
Yes. Korvanto can supply laterite in customized sizes and specifications based on customer requirements including particle size moisture chemical composition and packaging options suitable for the intended application.
