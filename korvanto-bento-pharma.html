Overview

Product Overview

KORVANTO BENTO PHARMA™ is a high-purity bentonite specially processed for pharmaceutical healthcare nutraceutical and personal care applications. Carefully selected and refined to ensure consistent quality it offers excellent adsorption suspension swelling and binding properties required in a wide range of formulations.

Its unique mineral structure and high surface area make it an effective suspending agent stabilizer binder thickening agent and rheology modifier in pharmaceutical preparations. The product is suitable for use in tablets capsules suspensions topical formulations herbal products and other healthcare applications where product stability and consistency are essential.

Manufactured under stringent quality controls KORVANTO BENTO PHARMA™ delivers reliable performance for pharmaceutical and allied industries.
